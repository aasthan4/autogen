FUNCTIONS USED IN INPUT :

Function for list_terms -
library/print_terms(list_terms): prints all terms in teh list_terms
library/convert_pqr(list_terms): converts general terms to all possible combinations of i,a
library/pick(list_terms,upper_form,lower_form)- picks terms from list terms which are npt fully contracted terms
library/compare_envelope - envelope of comparing list terms and returning the reduced form

Functions for general operators -
main_tools/product - product of 2 operators
main_tools/commutator - commutator of 2 operators

multi_cont(st1,st2,co1,co2): does contraction of 1 string of operators with other string of operators. central function in any GWT
